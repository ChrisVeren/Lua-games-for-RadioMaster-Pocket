HOW TO INSTALL<br /><br />

1 - Turn On your Pocket<br />
2 - Connect the top usb with your PC <br />
3 - Select on Your Pocket "USB Storage (SD)"<br />
4 - On your PC a new Drive should show up called "USB Driver", open it<br />
5 - Go to SCRIPTS -> TOOLS<br />
6 - And paste the Games in there<br />
7 - Eject the drive sefly<br />
8 - Remove the USB from your Pocket and the games should show up on your TOOLS menu<br />
9 - Enjoy!!<br />
