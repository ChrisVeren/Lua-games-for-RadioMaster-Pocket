HOW TO INSTALL

1 - Turn On your Pocket
2 - Connect the top usb with your PC 
3 - Select on Your Pocket "USB Storage (SD)"
4 - On your PC a new Drive should show up called "USB Driver", open it
5 - Go to SCRIPTS -> TOOLS
6 - And paste the Games in there
7 - Eject the drive sefly
8 - Remove the USB from your Pocket and the games should show up on your TOOLS menu
9 - Enjoy!!
